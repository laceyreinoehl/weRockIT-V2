Beyond the Talks

WeRockIT is here to offer more then just talks from industry leaders

Mother's Room

A Mother's Room dedicated to women who need a place to securely pump and store breastmilk. A signup schedule will be available at the registration table Friday morning. Volunteers will meet guests at the mother's room to let them in at registered time. A mini-fridge will be located within the room to store breast milk at 40°F.

Social Lounge

Want to network and meet other industry professionals? Visit the Social Lounge hosted by local business Fiber Artwork! Local organizations will have tables providing direct access to their members to so that attendees can learn about the work they do and how they can help.

Quiet Room

Need to relax and escape the conference environment? A Quiet Room has been reserved that will allow attendees to decompress. No talking allowed!

Social Level

When you purchase a ticket, tell your fellow guests your preferred level of social interaction through badge colors. Green means ready to mingle, yellow means neutral, and a red dot means no photos and no unsolicited business cards!

Upload Resume

WeRockIT Conf sponsors are excited to see resumes from WeRockIT Conf attendees. Upload your resume here.

Speed Mentorship

At sign in, remember to sign up for our Speed Mentorship Event. After the close of the last talk on Friday, meet with five mentors in just one hour. Get a new perspective, get a gut check, have a sounding board (or five!), and learn from other people’s experience. Bring your career questions!
