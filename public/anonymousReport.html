Anonymous CoC Violation Report

WeRockIT Conf takes these reports very seriously. We can't follow up an anonymous report with you directly, but we will fully investigate it and take whatever action is necessary to prevent a recurrence.

Please include as much of the following information as you feel comfortable sharing:

  • Identifying information (name/badge number) of the participant
  • The time you observed the incident
  • The behavior that was in violation
  • The circumstances surrounding the incident
  • Other people involved in the incident
