Keynote

Katreena Mullican

Director of Technologgy

HudsonAlpha Institute for Biotechnology

Mullican has been engineering Information Technology solutions for nearly a quarter century - since before clouds were cool. Her career began as a programming intern while still in college. She then mastered a variety of IT roles including System Administrator, Senior Linux System Administrator, Advanced Software Engineer, Cloud Engineer, DevOps Engineer, IT Consultant, Senior Architect, and Cloud Whisperer. Her IT expertise and experience, along with her advanced skill set, make her one of the most sought after individuals in Linux and cloud computing. Mullican has presented her automated hybrid cloud solutions to world-wide audiences.

Read More >>

At HudsonAlpha, a nonprofit genomics and genetics research institute, Mullican is responsible for managing the Information Technology team and infrastructure. The environment is a mix of on-premises and public cloud compute, storage, and network resources including physical and virtual servers, composable infrastructure, and hyper-converged systems. Mullican is known for her ability to rapidly master emergent hardware and software to create functional and agile solutions. Her team at HudsonAlpha is tasked with thinking outside the box to design hybrid platforms appropriate for the Institute’s research workloads as well as Institute associate companies that call HudsonAlpha’s 152-acre campus home.

Mullican received a bachelor’s degree in mathematics/computer science from Midland Lutheran College (now Midland University) and a master’s in information technology from Florida Institute of Technology. She has earned numerous IT certifications throughout her career in the areas of Linux, storage, and security. Mullican is a member of Phi Kappa Phi, Society of Women Engineers, and Women Who Code. She is a BuiltByGirls Wave mentor and strives to inspire students interested in STEM.

Mullican is an accomplished flautist and regularly performs with area ensembles. She has lived in Huntsville, Alabama for the past 23 years. Mullican is married to a Linux System Architect and they have an adult son who is a Chief Information Systems Officer.

Alain Lompo
Alain is a passion driven software developer with more than fifteen years of professional experience. he also worked previously as a technical trainer for Java/JEE and .Net technologies. he loves sharing amazing technologies in seminar, conferences and evangelism events. Alain regularly contribute to awesome open source projects on github (https://github.com/alainlompo) and helps developers everywhere to solve their problems via [stackoverflow](www.stackoverflow.com). Due to his contribution to technical communities, Alain was twice awarded the MVP (Microsoft Most Valuable Professional) title in 2006 and 2007. Alain is a MCSD .Net (Microsoft Certified Solution Developer), MCT (Microsoft Certified Trainer) since 2004. He is also a Certified Java Programmer and an Oracle Certified Web Component Developer (OCWCD). Alain is the initial architect and main developer of the meodes solution (www.meodes.com) and was, from 2006 to 2009 the technical expert for both .Net and Java/JEE platform for the european union project MEDA 2
Blakeley Kilgore
As a self-proclaimed “UX Unicorn” with strong development and coding skills, Blakeley Kilgore is continuously considering the merge between user-facing interfaces and the technologies behind them. Kilgore stepped into the web-development scene after a long affair with art and graphic design, allowing her natural creativity to make for a unique perspective in the sometimes bland world of coding. As a lifelong learner, she holds a B.A. in Liberal Arts from Auburn University and an M.A. in Web Design and Online Communication from the University of Florida. She currently works as a front-end web developer for Digium, a Sangoma company based in Huntsville, AL, and she is the lecturer for the course “Advanced Web Topics II: Programming and Specialized Topics” in the University of Florida’s graduate school. When Kilgore is not working in the online world, creating art, or eating avocado toast, you will find her outdoors, most likely near the water, and most likely with her dog.
